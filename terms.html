1. Service Description

ViewOnPhone (www.viewonphone.com) is a free, web-based tool that allows users to preview how any website appears on various mobile devices including smartphones and tablets. The service simulates device viewports and, where possible, proxies web content to render it inside a realistic device frame.

ViewOnPhone is provided free of charge. No account or registration is required to use the core features.
